Journalism

Carli Brosseau is a freelance journalist based in Chapel Hill, North Carolina. She brings expertise in data analysis and public records law to deep-dive projects and an investigative sensibility to breaking news. She has worked as a data reporter on the investigative teams at The Oregonian and the Arizona Daily Star and has extensive experience covering law enforcement.
