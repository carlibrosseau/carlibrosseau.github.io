Journalism

Carli Brosseau is an investigative reporter at The News & Observer in Raleigh, North Carolina. She brings expertise in data analysis and public records law to deep-dive projects and an investigative sensibility to breaking news. Prior to joining the N&O in 2018, Carli worked as a data reporter on the investigative teams at The Oregonian and the Arizona Daily Star. In 2020, she was a member of ProPublica's Local Reporting Network. Carli's work has been recognized with an Emmy, as well as awards from Investigative Reporters & Editors, Associated Press Media Editors and the Online News Association.
